    • INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT CAMEL

    • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes
    • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat
    • A camel does come across water, it can fill up in a hurry, drinking as much as 26 gallons in 10 minutes.

    • INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT CHAMELEON

    • Chameleons have almost 360 degree vision
    • Chameleons can't regrow their tails.
    • Chameleons have tiny holes on the sides of their head that are their "ears." The ear holes are so small that they are impossible to see to the naked eye.

    • INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT CICADA

    • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs
    • Cicadas have five eyes
    • Most cicadas have red-orange eyes.

    • INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT PANDA

    • An adult panda can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!
    • Pandas like to lick copper and iron.
    • Pandas have 6 toes to grasp bamboo.

    • INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT PIGEON

    • Pigeons can fly at altitudes up to and beyond 6000 feet.
    • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
    • hey can also recognise each letter of the human alphabet, differentiate between photographs, and even distinguish different humans within a photograph.

    • INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT BEAR

    • Most bears have 42 teeth.
    • There are only eight living species of bears.
    • A group of bears is called a sloth.
